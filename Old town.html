Old Town

Kaunas Old Town - the most established piece of Kaunas town, situated toward the east of the juncture of the Nemunas and Neris streams, involves 144 hectares. There are a ton of enduring Gothic, Renaissance and Baroque structures, particularly in the western piece of the Old Town. Banks, different workplaces, cafés, bistros, and numerous cutting edge shops are arranged in this road. It is a most loved spot for walk and gatherings of Kaunas citizens.
